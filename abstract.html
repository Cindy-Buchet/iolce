Abstract

First thing first we have to forget the history of Web : How Web was born? How things evolved? And why things are as they are today?

During Dave Shea’s conference in Düsseldorf in 2015 he decided to speak about the roots of Web to remind people why Web exist and why it is like that today. He divided his conference in three main topics: 1991 to 2000 (Far West), 2001 to 2006 (the quiet years) and 2007 to today.

{CSS}

The Web design exists since much more time than we think. But in the beginning it was used another way. In fact, it was not as powerful as it is nowadays.

At the beginning, the Web standards were not here. So, people had to manage with what they had. Most of the CSS properties were not existing. Because of that webdesigner had to rely on other ways to achieve their desires for their design. If they changed their browser Websites was displayed differently because every website was working with one particular browser.

Companies were fighting to release the best new features. For that, they were updating often their softwares.

Web lived a very long history. Bad things as good things happened to get to how it is today.
